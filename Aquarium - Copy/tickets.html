Tickets & Membership

Purchase tickets online in advance for the best value. General admission varies by date; select your visit day when you buy. Current rates and availability are always listed on vanaqua.org.

Member Benefits

Membership often pays for itself within a few visits and includes year-round perks such as:

  • Unlimited general admission for covered guests
  • Discounts at the gift shop and select dining
  • Member communications and presale opportunities
  • Individual, dual, and family tiers

Become a Member

  1. Open vanaqua.org.
  2. Select Become a Member.
  3. Choose your tier and complete your purchase.

Membership Levels

Tier Best for Highlights
